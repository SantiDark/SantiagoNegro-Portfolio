Process

How I work

A simple process that I apply both to game design work and to performance creatives.

Design process

  1. Research & references – understand goals, constraints and reference experiences.
  2. Define the core loop – what players do repeatedly and why it’s engaging.
  3. Prototype quickly – build the smallest slice that proves or disproves the idea.
  4. Test for readability – iterate on feedback, clarity and difficulty spikes.
  5. Document for the team – keep docs, diagrams and changelogs usable for others.

Creative / campaign process

  1. Clarify the offer – what we’re selling, to whom and in which context.
  2. Choose angles & hooks – emotion, social proof, FOMO, price, POV, etc.
  3. Design creatives – static, video or UGC adapted to placements and funnel stage.
  4. Test & learn – compare variations and measure what actually moves metrics.
  5. Refine & scale – keep what works, cut what doesn’t, plan the next round.
