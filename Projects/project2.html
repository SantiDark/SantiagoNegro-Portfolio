Stillness – Systemic Narrative Simulation

System design document · Trust, perception & memory · Role: Game Designer

Overview

Stillness explores how internal states like trust, fear and certainty shape both NPC and player behaviour over time. Instead of fixed narrative branches, the game models how characters update their perception of the player and the world based on what they see, remember and believe.

Design focus

  • Systemic approach to narrative rather than branching trees.
  • Trust and confidence lines that affect dialogue, access and tone.
  • States that react to context: environment, recent events, choices.
