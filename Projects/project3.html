Stealth Prototype – MGS-inspired

Level & systems prototype · Enemy states, patrol & vision cones · Role: Game Designer

Overview

A small stealth prototype inspired by classic Metal Gear Solid, focused on guard behaviour and level readability. The goal was to make the player understand at a glance when they are safe, about to be spotted or fully detected.

Design focus

  • Clear enemy state machine: patrol, suspicious, alert / chase.
  • Vision cones and occlusion logic with clean feedback.
  • Level layout that supports sneaking, flanking and planning routes.
